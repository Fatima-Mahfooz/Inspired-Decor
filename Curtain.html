Sheer/Linen

In addition to providing privacy from the sun, curtains enhance the aesthetics of your windows. Any environment is made more stylish and sophisticated by the delicate, airy texture of linen. Sheer curtains look fantastic draped over heavier drapes and can provide subtle decoration to your windows.This material is available in an extensive array of hues, designs, and patterns to complement any kind of home and style preference..

Velvet/Polyester

Velvet curtains and drapes are easy to find at Inspired Decor. Whether you are choosing velvet for function or style, these drapes are sure to look great in your home. Jacquard curtains

Made from luxurious jacquard fabric, jacquard curtains have elaborate woven designs that radiate sophistication and beauty. These thick, long-lasting drapes give any room an air of royalty. Inspired Decor provides an exquisite selection of patterns on jacquard textiles, such as animal prints, paisleys, and flowers. Our selection of jacquard curtains simply elevates your home décor by combining classic beauty and functionality, making them ideal for individuals looking to manage light and improve privacy.
