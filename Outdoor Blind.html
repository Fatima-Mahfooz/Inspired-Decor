Features of Outdoor & Allusion Blinds


Free Hang Blinds

You can motorize your blind or use a detachable crank handle to raise and lower it. A variety of hold-down clips are used to fix the blind at the bottom, allowing it to stay down in windy conditions. Because a Free Hang patio blind shields you from the sun's heat and glare, it's a great way to make more use of your outdoor leisure spaces.
Tips to note:where the hold-down method cannot be used to keep the blind in place when it is fully lowered and if the outdoor space needs complete weather protection (there are other solutions available).

Channel X Blinds

Slidetrack blinds, sometimes referred to as Channel X outdoor blinds, have side channels that enclose the blind. This results in a wall-to-wall finish, improved insulation, and weather protection, as well as a safer way to raise and lower the blind.Channel X is good for providing some weather protection for your outdoor space while also reducing sun and glare.
Tips to note:Steer clear of apertures that aren't precisely square or that have skirtings or stepped tiles blocking them. It should be noted that the cloth may occasionally be blown out of the channel by strong winds, thus it cannot always be kept inside.
