Spring Ideas To Inspired Your Home Decor


Incorporate Change To Your OUT

As the weather becomes warmer, you can swap out your bulky pillows and throw blankets with lighter linens. The abundance of neutral-toned cushions in Shea McGee's 2024 spring collection, which is currently on display, will add a touch of springtime elegance to any space. Shea McGee of Studio McGee says, "Layering in a lighter-toned rug and pillows for a touch of coziness will imbue your space with new, revitalizing energy, ahead of summer."

LET NATURE'S CRITTERS IN.

Once more, we find ourselves back in Sheila Bridges's bright, quirky Hudson Valley getaway. Our most cherished detail? A collection of hand-carved birds in lovely glass cloches sit on an antique Swedish table in the living area. There isn't a more perfect (or dare we say, tweeter?) springtime ode to sunny days to come.

Roses in a Wicker Basket

We start by taking a large wicker basket and filling it to the brim with  pink flower stems. This basket can be set on the floor at the front door or on top of the console table in your foyer. An elegant way to adorn your front door for spring is with a spring wreath. A spring wreath may be used in so many different parts of your house!

Floral wreath and botanical wall art

I adore this lovely mantel for the spring fireplace! It's likely a collection of little floral artworks that have been placed next to one another to make a lovely botanical collage of flowers! At first, I thought there was some kind of botanical tile art above the fireplace. This botanical collage includes a floral hoop wreath with pink roses hung right in the middle, making the ideal spring fireplace vignette! A few of eucalyptus leaves rest at the base of each of the silver candles that surround the fireplace mantel on either side.
